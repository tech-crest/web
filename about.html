✦ Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Education & Industry

TechCrest Learning was founded with one clear goal — to close the massive gap between what colleges teach and what the industry actually demands. We saw too many talented engineers struggle not because of lack of potential, but because of lack of the right, practical skills.

Every course we design is built in close collaboration with industry professionals. Every project is real-world. Every mentor has walked the path our students are walking. We do not just teach — we prepare.

✦ Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small mentoring group to a full-fledged tech education platform.

2021

The Beginning

TechCrest started as a small WhatsApp group of engineers mentoring fresh graduates on Embedded Systems fundamentals. Word spread fast.

2022

First Batch Launched

We launched our first structured Embedded Systems batch with 20 students. 18 of them got placed within 3 months. We knew we were onto something.

2023

Expanding the Curriculum

Added IoT, PCB Design, and DSA tracks. Crossed 200 enrolled students. Built our first dedicated lab facility for hands-on hardware training.

2024

MERN Stack & Web Dev

Launched the MERN Stack Development course in response to overwhelming demand. Crossed 400 students trained and achieved a 95% placement rate.

2025

TechCrest 2.0

Revamped all courses with updated curriculum, launched this new platform, and set a goal to train 1000+ students by year end. The journey continues.
