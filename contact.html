✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

Do I need prior experience to enroll in your courses?

+
Most of our courses are designed for beginners and do not require any prior experience. The Embedded Systems, IoT, PCB Design, and MERN Stack courses all start from the very basics. For DSA, basic knowledge of any programming language is helpful but not mandatory — we cover everything from scratch.

Are the classes online or offline?

+
We offer both online and offline batches. Online classes are conducted via Zoom/Google Meet with recorded sessions available for revision. Offline batches are held at our Bengaluru facility. You can choose what works best for you at the time of enrollment.

What happens after I click "Enroll Now"?

+
When you click Enroll Now on any course, you fill in a quick form with your name, email, and phone. You are then redirected to our WhatsApp with a pre-filled message that includes your details and the course name. Our team reviews it and contacts you within 1 hour to confirm your seat and guide you on payment and batch schedule.

Do you offer EMI or instalment payment options?

+
Yes, we offer flexible payment options including 2–3 instalment plans for all courses. Please mention this when you contact us on WhatsApp or via this form and we will set it up for you without any additional charges.

Will I get a certificate after completing the course?

+
Yes. Every student who completes the course and the final project receives a TechCrest Learning certificate of completion. This certificate is digitally signed and includes your course details, duration, and skills covered. It can be shared on LinkedIn and added to your resume.

How does placement support work?

+
Our placement support includes resume building, LinkedIn profile optimization, mock technical interviews, and direct referrals to our hiring partner companies. We stay connected with you even after course completion until you land your first or next role. Our current placement rate stands at 95%.

How long do I have access to course materials?

+
You get lifetime access to all course materials, recorded sessions, notes, and project files. Whenever we update the course content with new topics or tools, you automatically get access to those updates at no extra cost.

Still Have Questions?

The fastest way to get answers is on WhatsApp. Our team is online Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM.
