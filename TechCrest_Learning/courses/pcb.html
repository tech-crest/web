🎯 What You Will Learn

✓Schematic capture with KiCad and Altium
✓Component selection and footprint assignment

📌 Requirements

Basic electronics knowledge (resistors, capacitors, ICs)
A laptop with at least 8GB RAM (for Altium)
KiCad is free — Altium trial license provided during course
Curiosity to design real boards — no CAD experience needed
💡

You will physically fabricate a PCB. Students who complete the course will receive guidance on ordering their capstone project board from a manufacturer at discounted rates.

📚 Course Syllabus

W1
PCB Fundamentals & KiCad Setup
5 topics · 4 hrs
+
What is a PCB? Layers, substrates and copper traces
PCB manufacturing process overview
Installing and navigating KiCad 7
KiCad project structure: schematic + PCB editor
Your first schematic: simple LED circuit
W2
Schematic Capture & Symbol Libraries
6 topics · 5 hrs
+
Adding and connecting components in schematic
Using and creating custom symbol libraries
Power symbols, labels and hierarchical sheets
Netlist generation and annotation
Electrical Rule Check (ERC) and fixing errors
Project: Arduino Uno shield schematic
W3–4
PCB Layout, Routing & Ground Planes
8 topics · 8 hrs
+
Footprint assignment and component placement
PCB layers: F.Cu, B.Cu, Silkscreen, Courtyard
Manual routing vs interactive router
Trace width calculation for current capacity
Ground planes: copper pour and thermal reliefs
Via types: through-hole, blind, buried
Design Rule Check (DRC) setup and resolution
Project: 2-layer power supply PCB layout
W5
Altium Designer Introduction
5 topics · 5 hrs
+
Altium Designer interface and project setup
Schematic and PCB in Altium workflow
Altium component libraries and Vault
Interactive routing and differential pairs
Comparing KiCad vs Altium for industry use
W6
Multi-Layer & High-Speed PCB Design
5 topics · 5 hrs
+
4-layer PCB stackup planning
Signal integrity: impedance matching and return paths
EMI/EMC considerations in PCB layout
Decoupling capacitor placement strategies
USB, HDMI and high-speed trace routing
W7–8
Fabrication, BOM & Capstone Project
6 topics · 8 hrs
+
Generating Gerber files and drill files
Bill of Materials (BOM) generation
Ordering from JLCPCB / PCBWay with SMT assembly
Capstone: Design a microcontroller development board
Portfolio presentation of completed PCB designs
Career paths in PCB design and EDA tool industry
